Full codebase health assessment.

**Input needed:**

- Focus areas (optional)

**Output:**
- Health score
- Issue inventory by severity
- Technical debt catalog
- Prioritized action plan

**Use when:**
- Project health check
- Inheriting a codebase
- Quarterly reviews
