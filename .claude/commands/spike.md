# /spike - Technical Research

Load and execute: `C:\scripts\prompts\execution\SPIKE_RESEARCH.md`

## Quick Start

Time-boxed technical research to answer unknowns.

**Input needed:**
- Question to answer
- Time box (default: 30 min)
- Success criteria

**Output:**
- Research findings
- Recommendation
- Prototype (if applicable)
- Decision ready for ADR

**Use when:**
- Hit an unknown during implementation
- Evaluating technology choices
- Need proof-of-concept before committing
